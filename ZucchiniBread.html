Zucchini Bread

Welcome to the delightful world of zucchini bread!

This wholesome and delicious treat is a perfect blend of moist, flavorful goodness and a delightful way to make use of abundant zucchinis from your garden or local market. Bursting with natural sweetness and a subtle hint of spice, zucchini bread is a beloved classic that never fails to bring comfort and joy to those who indulge in.

The recipe we'll be exploring is a timeless favorite, cherished by generations for its simplicity and heartwarming taste. The tender crumb, adorned with little pockets of grated zucchini, creates a moist texture that's simply irresistible. Whether you're a seasoned baker or a newcomer to the world of baking, this zucchini bread recipe is a breeze to master, promising a delightful experience for your taste buds and a wonderful aroma that fills your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded zucchini

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F, grease an 8x4 inch or 9x5 inch loaf ban with nonsticking cooking spray or butter
  2. Add oil, both sugars, eggs, and vanilla extract to a large bowl. Stir until smooth.
  3. Mix in cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder and salt.
  4. Stir in flour, mixture will be thick. Add zucchini and stir until combined – mixture will get looser and more pourable after stirring in zucchini.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake 50-60 minutes (9×5-inch pan) or 55-65 minutes (8×4-inch) pan, or until a toothpick comes out clean from the center of the loaf.
  6. Cool before slicing or removing from the pan. Store covered for up to 2 days or freeze for up to 3 months.
